# Inter-Company Material Requisition & Logistics Automation

## 📌 Project Title

**Inter-Company Material Requisition & Logistics Automation**

***

## 👨‍💼 Role

**Software Tester (ERP Domain)**

***

## 📝 Project Description

This project involves an **inter-company business process automation** between two group companies operating under a common parent organization.

The objective of the project is to automate the **material requisition, approval workflow, inter-company purchasing and sales transactions, and logistics planning**.

The process starts with **Material Requisition (MR)** and follows a structured approval workflow. Upon final logistics approval, the system automatically generates **Purchase Orders (PO)** and corresponding **Sales Orders (SO)** across group companies.

Further, **Advance Planning and Scheduling (APS)** ensures optimized vehicle utilization and accurate material dispatch planning.

This automation minimizes manual intervention, improves traceability, and ensures seamless coordination between execution and manufacturing units.

***

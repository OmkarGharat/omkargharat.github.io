# ERP Implementation

**📝 Project Section Format:**

**Project Name:** ERP Implementation for a Leading Furniture Manufacturer

**Role:** ERP Functional Tester & Implementation Specialist

**Duration:** \[May 2025] - \[June 2025]

**Location:** Client Site (Onsite)

**Project Description:**

* Executed end-to-end implementation of ERP modules for the **Purchase and Sales Cycles**.
* Configured critical modules:
  * **Purchase Cycle:** Purchase Order (PO), Goods Receipt Note (GRN), Purchase Bill Booking, Expense Bill Booking, Inventory Receipt, Inventory Issue.
  * **Sales Cycle:** Sales Order, Sales Order Approval, Sales Invoice.
* Delivered **onsite training** to users, ensuring seamless adoption and effective utilization of ERP features.
* Actively addressed client queries and ensured optimal usage of the configured modules.
* Enhanced user engagement through **comprehensive documentation** and **hands-on training sessions**.

***

**Key Achievements:**

* Successfully implemented ERP solutions for a **leading furniture manufacturer**, achieving a **smooth transition and positive user feedback**.
* Demonstrated proficiency in both **ERP Testing and Implementation**, showcasing adaptability in client-driven environments.
* Achieved high **client satisfaction** by delivering **customized training** and efficient module configuration.
